# IPL Player Profiles & Career Statistics

This document contains comprehensive player statistics, career milestones, historical facts, and trivia for key Indian Premier League (IPL) players.

## MS Dhoni (Chennai Super Kings)
- **Role**: Wicketkeeper-Batsman & Captain
- **IPL Matches**: 264 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 5,243 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 137.54
- **IPL Batting Average**: 39.12
- **Trophies Won**: 5 trophies as captain of Chennai Super Kings (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Led CSK to 10 finals and 12 playoffs, the most by any captain.
  - Held the record for the most dismissals as a wicketkeeper in IPL (over 180 dismissals including catches and stumpings).
  - Known as the "Ultimate Finisher", hitting the most runs in the 20th over of IPL matches (over 700 runs in the final over).
  - Has hit 252 sixes in his IPL career.
  - Played for Rising Pune Supergiant in 2016 and 2017 when CSK was suspended. Led RPS to the final in 2017.
  - Played his 250th IPL match in the 2023 final against Gujarat Titans.
  - Scored a career-best of 84* against Royal Challengers Bangalore in 2019.

## Virat Kohli (Royal Challengers Bangalore)
- **Role**: Top-Order Batsman
- **IPL Matches**: 252 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 8,004 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 131.97
- **IPL Batting Average**: 38.66
- **Centuries**: 8 centuries (the most in IPL history).
- **Half-Centuries**: 55 half-centuries.
- **Key Achievements**:
  - All-time leading run-scorer in IPL history, being the first player to cross 8,000 runs.
  - Scored 973 runs in a single IPL season (2016), which is the record for the most runs in a single tournament.
  - Hit 4 centuries in the 2016 season alone.
  - Captained RCB from 2013 to 2021, leading them to the finals in 2016.
  - Has hit 272 sixes and 705 fours in his IPL career.
  - Only player to play for a single franchise (RCB) in all IPL seasons from 2008 to present.
  - Scored 113 against Kings XI Punjab in 2016 off just 50 balls despite having stitches in his left hand.

## Rohit Sharma (Mumbai Indians)
- **Role**: Opening Batsman & Captain
- **IPL Matches**: 257 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 6,628 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 131.14
- **IPL Batting Average**: 29.72
- **Centuries**: 2 centuries.
- **Half-Centuries**: 43 half-centuries.
- **Trophies Won**: 6 trophies total (5 as MI Captain in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020; and 1 as a player for Deccan Chargers in 2009).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - One of the most successful captains in IPL history with 5 titles.
  - Scored a century (109*) against Kolkata Knight Riders at Eden Gardens in 2012.
  - Has hit 280 sixes in his IPL career, ranking among the highest.
  - Took an IPL hat-trick for Deccan Chargers against Mumbai Indians in 2009.
  - Captained Mumbai Indians from 2013 to 2023 before Hardik Pandya took over the captaincy in 2024.

## Jasprit Bumrah (Mumbai Indians)
- **Role**: Fast Bowler
- **IPL Matches**: 133 matches
- **Wickets Taken**: 165 wickets
- **IPL Economy Rate**: 7.30
- **IPL Bowling Average**: 22.51
- **Best Bowling Figures**: 5/10 against Kolkata Knight Riders in 2022.
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Renowned as one of the best death bowlers in cricket history.
  - Won 5 IPL championships with Mumbai Indians.
  - Consistently bowls at speeds exceeding 140 km/h with high accuracy in yorkers.
  - Took 27 wickets in the 2020 season, helping MI win the championship.
  - Made his IPL debut in 2013, taking 3/32 against RCB, including the wicket of Virat Kohli.

## Yuzvendra Chahal (Rajasthan Royals)
- **Role**: Leg-Spinner
- **IPL Matches**: 160 matches
- **Wickets Taken**: 205 wickets (the most in IPL history).
- **IPL Economy Rate**: 7.84
- **IPL Bowling Average**: 22.44
- **Best Bowling Figures**: 5/40 against Kolkata Knight Riders in 2022 (which included a hat-trick).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - All-time leading wicket-taker in IPL history.
  - Played key years for Royal Challengers Bangalore (2014-2021) before joining Rajasthan Royals in 2022.
  - Won the Purple Cap in IPL 2022 with 27 wickets.
  - Known for his tactical intelligence, flighting the ball to deceive batsmen.

## Chris Gayle (Punjab Kings / RCB / KKR)
- **Role**: Opening Batsman (The "Universe Boss")
- **IPL Matches**: 142 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 4,965 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 148.96
- **IPL Batting Average**: 39.72
- **Centuries**: 6 centuries.
- **Sixes Hit**: 357 sixes (the most in IPL history).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Scored the highest individual score in IPL history: 175* off 66 balls against Pune Warriors India at Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bangalore in 2013.
  - Reached his century in that match off just 30 balls, the fastest century in IPL.
  - Won the Orange Cap consecutively in 2011 and 2012 for RCB.
  - Hit 17 sixes in his 175* innings, which is the record for most sixes in an individual innings.
  - Scored 733 runs in the 2012 season.

## AB de Villiers (Royal Challengers Bangalore / Delhi Daredevils)
- **Role**: Middle-Order Batsman (Mr. 360)
- **IPL Matches**: 184 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 5,162 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 151.68
- **IPL Batting Average**: 39.70
- **Centuries**: 3 centuries.
- **Half-Centuries**: 40 half-centuries.
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Known for his ability to hit the ball in all parts of the ground (360 degrees).
  - Won the most Player of the Match awards in IPL history (25 awards).
  - Recorded a 215-run partnership with Virat Kohli against Mumbai Indians in 2015, which is one of the highest partnerships.
  - Scored a career-best 133* against Mumbai Indians in 2015.
  - Inducted into the RCB Hall of Fame after his retirement in 2021.

## Sunil Narine (Kolkata Knight Riders)
- **Role**: All-Rounder / Off-Spinner
- **IPL Matches**: 177 matches
- **Wickets Taken**: 180 wickets
- **IPL Economy Rate**: 6.73 (one of the best in IPL history)
- **Runs Scored**: 1,532 runs
- **IPL Batting Strike Rate**: 162.88
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Has won 3 IPL titles with Kolkata Knight Riders (2012, 2014, 2024).
  - Named Most Valuable Player (MVP) of the tournament three times (2012, 2018, 2024), the most by any player.
  - Recorded the joint fastest half-century in IPL off 15 balls against RCB in 2017.
  - Transformed from a specialist bowler to an explosive pinch-hitter opener.
  - Took 24 wickets in his debut season in 2012 to lead KKR to their first title.

## Andre Russell (Kolkata Knight Riders)
- **Role**: All-Rounder
- **IPL Matches**: 127 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 2,484 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 174.00 (highest career strike rate in IPL history for players with >1000 runs).
- **Wickets Taken**: 115 wickets
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Named MVP of the tournament twice (2015, 2019).
  - Hit 52 sixes in the 2019 season, single-handedly winning several matches.
  - Scored 88* off 36 balls against CSK in 2018.
  - Took a 5-wicket haul (5/15) against Mumbai Indians in just 2 overs in 2021.
  - Key member of the KKR championship-winning team in 2014 and 2024.

## David Warner (Delhi Capitals / Sunrisers Hyderabad)
- **Role**: Opening Batsman
- **IPL Matches**: 184 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 6,565 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 139.77
- **IPL Batting Average**: 40.52
- **Centuries**: 4 centuries.
- **Half-Centuries**: 62 half-centuries (the most in IPL history).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Led Sunrisers Hyderabad to their maiden IPL trophy in 2016, scoring 848 runs in that season.
  - The only player to win the Orange Cap three times (2015, 2017, 2019).
  - Highest run-scorer among foreign (overseas) players in IPL history.
  - Scored 126 against KKR in 2017.

## Suresh Raina (Chennai Super Kings / Gujarat Lions)
- **Role**: Middle-Order Batsman (Mr. IPL)
- **IPL Matches**: 205 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 5,528 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 136.76
- **IPL Batting Average**: 32.51
- **Key Achievements**:
  - The first player to reach 5,000 runs in IPL.
  - Known as "Mr. IPL" due to his consistent performance year after year.
  - Held the record for playing 158 consecutive matches for CSK.
  - Scored a blistering 87 runs off just 25 balls against Kings XI Punjab in Qualifier 2 in 2014.
  - Took 109 catches in the field, one of the highest by an outfielder.
  - Captained Gujarat Lions in 2016 and 2017 when CSK was suspended.

## Hardik Pandya (Mumbai Indians / Gujarat Titans)
- **Role**: Pace-Bowling All-Rounder
- **IPL Matches**: 137 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 2,525 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 143.43
- **Wickets Taken**: 64 wickets
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Led debutants Gujarat Titans to the IPL title in 2022 as captain and to the finals in 2023.
  - Won 4 IPL titles as a player with Mumbai Indians (2015, 2017, 2019, 2020).
  - Returned to Mumbai Indians in 2024 as captain, replacing Rohit Sharma.
  - Known for explosive finishing batting and crucial medium-fast bowling spells.

## KL Rahul (Lucknow Super Giants / Punjab Kings / RCB)
- **Role**: Opening Batsman & Wicketkeeper
- **IPL Matches**: 132 matches
- **Runs Scored**: 4,683 runs
- **IPL Strike Rate**: 134.60
- **IPL Batting Average**: 45.46 (one of the highest career averages in IPL history).
- **Key Achievements**:
  - Won the Orange Cap in 2020 scoring 670 runs for Kings XI Punjab.
  - Scored the fastest fifty in IPL history (off 14 balls) against Delhi Daredevils in 2018 (since matched by Yashasvi Jaiswal in 13 balls).
  - Captain of Lucknow Super Giants since their inception in 2022, leading them to playoffs in 2022 and 2023.
  - Scored 4 IPL centuries.
